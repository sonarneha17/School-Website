Welcome to ZP Primary Girls School

Z P Primary Girls' school was established on 5th August 1922 at Main Road at Kalwan. Initially there were only few students, but as the years have been passed by, the count is reached more than 4000 students till now. Now the shrub of Z P Primary Girls' school have been grown into big tree. Accomodating over 4000 students in various mediums consisting Marathi and Semi. Having a good qualified and experienced teaching staff makes our students stand out

What We Offer

The students' safety is the most important thing when they are in school. That's why our school provides safety measures first, then regular classes, best teachers to conduct classes. The atmosphere which surrounds these small kids should be fresh and ambigious for their mind. So there are neatly cleaned and sufficient classrooms, some creative lessons and also sports facilities too.
